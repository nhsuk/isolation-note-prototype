You do not need an isolation note

You can only get an isolation note if:

  • you have symptoms of coronavirus or have tested positive
  • you live with someone who has symptoms or has tested positive, and you’re not fully vaccinated
  • you’ve been told to self-isolate by NHS Test and Trace or the NHS COVID-19 app

England: advice for people at higher risk on the NHS website

Scotland: shielding advice on NHS inform

Northern Ireland: guidance for clinically extremely vulnerable people on nidirect
